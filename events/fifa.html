RULES

  1. GAME MODE:
  2. For ease of use all games will be played in “Kick Off” Mode.
  3. PICKING TEAMS:
  4. Every player is allowed to pick their preferred team. Teams can be picked before the start of the game. Players are allowed to use multiple teams throughout the tournament as they please. It is accepted to have the same teams play against each other (e.g. Barcelona). In that case, the ‘away’ player will have to pick the Away Jersey for that team. In general, ‘away’ players are responsible for picking the jersey that does not resemble the home team jersey.
  5. SCORE RECORDING:
  6. The scores will be reported to the score table by both players after the game.
  7. GAME-PLAY SETTINGS:
  8. 6-minute Halves
  9. Injuries: On
  10. Off Sides: On
  11. Time/Score Display: On
  12. Player Status Bar: Player Names
  13. Camera: Dynamic or Tele
  14. MATCH SCHEDULING:
  15. Each player will play a single-elimination game. After single-elimination stage the winner will advance to the next level and so on up to the final.
  16. GENERAL RULES:
  17. All games will be played on the PC
  18. Frequent and purposeful pausing during game play may be subject to disqualification.
  19. If a player purposely turns off a game while it’s in progress, that player will be disqualified from the tournament. Entry into future tournaments may be denied.
  20. All players must play the Game using the above game-play settings. Any deviation from these settings will result in automatic disqualification. If game settings are found to be wrong by the tournament administrators, the players and the game will be disqualified.
  21. 7. The administration has the right to disqualify players from the tournament after misconduct or unethical behaviour during the Tournament. Disqualification means that the player is not allowed to play any more games in the Tournament.
