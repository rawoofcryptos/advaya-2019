DESCRIPTION

      Soldering is an art of placing and fixing the components neatly.The participants are instructed to solder the given ciruit to general purpose PCB.Judgement will be based on how fast and neatly the PCB being soldered.

RULES

  1. The competition runs for sixty minutes or until first person finishes the board kit.
  2. There will be elimination rounds if necessary.
  3. There will be second round if there is any tie in the first round.
  4. Six participants will be selected through screening tests.
  5. Electronic components and soldering iron will be provided.
  6. The faster you finish the work neatly, the easier you will bag the prize.
  7. Any misbehavior during the event can lead to elimination of the team.
  8. Decision of the judges will be final and binding.
