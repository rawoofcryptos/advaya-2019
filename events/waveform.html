DESCRIPTION

      This is a group event and all the groups are instructed to reproduce the same waveform as given. The fastest group which is obtained the waveform accurately within the time limit is subjected to the prize.

RULES

  1. Maximum number of participants allowed in one team is two(2).
  2. Duration of the event is 2 hours.
  3. Electronic components and instruments will be provided
  4. Participants are not permitted to have mobile phones and any other electronic equipments.
  5. The participants are instructed to generate the given waveform, by using circuit diagram.
  6. The waveform is given on the spot.
  7. Each participant can't take more than three chances, negative marks will be awarded for the second and third submission.
  8. There will be second round if there is any tie in the first round.
  9. The faster you finish the work neatly, the easier you will bag the prize.
  10. Any misbehavior during the event can lead to elimination of the team.
  11. Decision of the judges will be final and binding.
