DESCRIPTION

       In the area of E & E circuits, the occurence of errors are common and is a complicated task to debug the errors in the circuits. Here we are providing opportunities for students to express their talent in the field of E & E circuit troubleshooting.

RULES

  1. Each team can have a maximum of two members.
  2. This event will be conducted in 2 phases-Prelims and Finals.
  3. 5 Teams will be selected for final.
  4. The finalists will be selected based on time basis.
  5. Calculators and mobile phones are not allowed.
  6. Decision of the judges will be final and binding.
Prelims :
  • Preliminaries will be written test with 15 question (Objective type and circuits with error in it).
  • Time duration is 30 minutes.
Finals :
  • This is a practical section. The 5 teams selected will be given a circuit with a error in it. The task is to find it.
  • Time duration is 1 hour.
