DESCRIPTION

      All the singers, it is time to get ready for a golden opportunity, as "VOICE OF GECI " challenge is coming your way.You have the chance to exhibit your singing skill..!!

RULES

  1. Rounds-2 auditions & final
  2. FOR 1ST ROUND
  3. Participants are required to perform vocal. Live auditions are mandatory for all participants.
  4. Mappilapattu, album songs, etc are not allowed.
  5. Time alloted for each performance is 3-5 minutes.
  6. Participants will be judged om the basis of song selection, voice quality, clarity, rhythm and their appeal to audience.
  7. Judges can stop the performance any time.
  8. Shortlistedcontestants will advance to semi-finals.
  9. SEMI-FINALS
  10. Participants will be given performance time of 5 minutes.
  11. Participants must perform with a karaoke track.
  12. Karaoke will not be provided, it must be brought by the participants.
  13. Decision of the judges will be final. Five contestants will be shortlisted and directly enter into final round.
