RULES

  1. The robots build by given dimension and specification will be allowed for game.
  2. The robot which gain maximum points will be the winner.
  3. The team should not consist of more than 4 members.
  4. Each member from same college is not mandatory.
  5. The robot should follow the robot specifications provided. Any deviation from the mentioned specifications will lead to disqualification.
  6. Once the race begins, three hand touches are allowed, if your taking hand touch you have to start from last check point.
  7. No test practice will be allowed on the main arena.
  8. Terminals for charging the battery will not be provided in the college.
  9. The arena may subject to change before the commencement of any round.
  10. If the robot crosses a checkpoint, and moves off track, then the robot would be placed back on the previous checkpoint crossed.
  11. The decision of the judges will be final and abiding. Argument with judges in any form will lead to the disqualification of the team.
  12. Lego kits are strictly not allowed in any form.
  13. The l x b x h should not exceed 25 x 25 x 25cm.
  14. The net weight should not exceed 3 kgs.
  15. Wired or wireless robot are allowed.
  16. The team should contain 2 to 4 members.
  17. The structure of the robot should not be changed during the competition.
  18. If the robot by any chances falls from the bridge, the robot will be kept back to the previous checkpoint crossed with a penalty.
  19. During the game play, if any part of a robot is destructed/ dismantled/ damaged the participant will be given a timeout to repair at an expense of a penalty, while the next participant for will be called the play.
  20. No readjustment is allowed during the run.
