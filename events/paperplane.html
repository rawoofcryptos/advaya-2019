DESCRIPTION

      Whether you create paper planes for fun, or have more professional expertise to creating the perfect paper plane, this friendly fun event will have something for everyone. Create your plane then test it against othersto find out who is the ultimate paper plane champion !!!

RULES

  1. Solo participant event.
  2. Duratioin of planee making is 10 minutes.
  3. Paper will be provided on spot.
  4. The sheet must be modified by folding and ripping only. No gluing or stapling is allowed.
  5. Pre-prepare planes will not allowed.
  6. Two trails per individual. Best of two counts.
  7. The plane must be launched behind a staight line on the floor.
  8. A run or fastwalk as part of launch is not allowed.
  9. The distance measured from the straight line to furthermost part of plane after it has come to rest.
  10. Planes may strike inanimate object along the way. WWe measure the horizontal displacement.
  11. Decision of the judges will be final and binding.
