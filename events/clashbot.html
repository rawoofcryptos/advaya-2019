DESCRIPTION

      Get ready to be involved in the "Clash Of Bots.." Ignite the fury inside thy, to knock out your enemy (keep an eyes on yours too). Its all about who is left at the end.

RULES

  1. Team Specification:
  2. A team may consist of a maximum of 4 participants, all from the same institute or may be from different institute
  3. General Rules:
  4. The maximum duration of each round will be 6 minutes. Any team that is not ready at the time specified will be disqualified from the competition automatically
  5. The machine would be checked for its safety before the competition and would be discarded if found unsafe for other participants and spectators.
  6. The organizers reserve the rights to change any or all of the above rules as they deem fit. Change in rules, if any will be highlighted on the website and notified to the registered teams.
  7. Violation of any the above rules will lead to disqualification.
  8. Judges' decision shall be treated as final and binding on all.
  9. Criteria for Triumph:
  10. A robot is declared victorious if its opponent is immobilized
  11. . A robot will be declared immobile if it cannot display linear motion of at least one inch in a timed period of 60 seconds. A bot with one side of its drive train disabled will not be counted out if it can demonstrate some degree of controlled movement.
  12. If both robots survive the five minutes at that point, the robot with the higher hit points wins
  13. Mobility:
  14. All bots must have easily visible and controlled mobility in order to compete. Methods of mobility include:
  15. Rolling (wheels, tracks or the whole robot).
  16. Jumping and hopping is not allowed.
  17. Flying (using airfoil, helium balloons, ornithopters, etc.) is not allowed
  18. Use of damaged, non-leak proof batteries may lead to disqualification.
  19. SCORE DISTRIBUTION
  20. IMMOBILIZE (5 SEC) - 2 POINTS
  21. FLIP- BOT 1 FLIP UPSIDE DOWN BOT 2
  22. If bot2 gains its position back WITHOUT touch by operator – Bot 1 will get - 3 points
  23. WITH touch by operator - Bot 1 will get – 5 points
  24. If any robot goes out of the court in any manner (like flipping over the court bar, or pushing through the entry slot) the opponent will get – 10 points
  25. GENERAL RULES
  26. The weight of the robot should be below 22kg..negotiable upto max 1kg
  27. The dimensions should be within (75*75*100)cm.
  28. External power supply should be a dc of max 48 volts
  29. Max of 4 members in each group.
  30. Weapons that can cause harm to audience and participants are strictly forbidden . eg:flame, throwers ,blades, electric shocks etc are not allowed
  31. Maximum allowed pressure is 12 bars.
  32. Teams can call for a repair time of 60seconds in between the war.
  33. A team can take only one repair time in one round.
  34. We are not responsible for any damages caused to the robot.
  35. ALL THESE RULES ARE APLICABLE TO ALL TYPE OF ROBOTS PARTICIPATING IN CLASHBOT.
  36. ANY TYPE OF FOUL OR FIGHT WITH JUDGES LEADS TO DISQUALIFICATION .
