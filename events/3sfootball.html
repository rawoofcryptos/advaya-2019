DESCRIPTION

       "Show your skills and score the goal." The speed of attack, the use of triangulation, and the strategies bring aspects of hockey into the game. The quick shift from one end of the field to the otherafter a goal is scored, or upon loss of possesion is reminiscent of basketball.

RULES

  1. Each team will have to defend the goal on its side as well as score the goal on the opposite side.
  2. Each team can have a maximum of 4 team members (including 1 sub).
  3. Duration of the game will be 6 min (each half will be 3 min).
  4. Substitution will be rolling.
  5. Only ground passes are allowed.
  6. Goals are allowed only from centre or after centre.
  7. If match is drawn,3 penalty will be allowed for each team, Penalty is taken from centre.
  8. Goal kick, Freekick, throw, corner will be taken only by one step.
  9. Any act of misbehaviour or misconduct will lead to immediate disqualification of the team.
  10. The decision taken by the refereewillbe final.
