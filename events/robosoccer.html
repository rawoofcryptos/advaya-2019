DESCRIPTION

      "Through my foot don't move, my intelligence does!!" More around to shoot the best in the opponents goal post with firing heros, and keep an eye behind to protect your goal post at the right move of seconds hand. Let get ready for the knockout soccer tournment with your bots.

RULES

  1. Each team will have to defend the goal on its side as well as score the goal on the opposite side.
  2. Each team can have a maximum of 3 team members.
  3. The weight limit of the robot for this competition is 5 Kg.
  4. Each robot should not Exceed by maximum 30cm.
  5. The controller and batteries should not exceed 5 kg (This means that whatever components the participants enter on to the field should not be more than 5kg).
  6. The game will start at the count of 3 given by refree followed by whistle. In case a team starts robot before the whistle, the game will be restarted and a team making this mistake for more than 3 times will be disqualified.
  7. Each goal scored by a team by rolling the ball on the ground will fetch the team 1 point.
  8. Maximum 2 members per team will be allowed to remain close to field for operating the robots.
  9. In case of jam up of robots for more than 30 seconds the robots will have to kick-off again at the order of the refree.
  10. In case of any discrepancy the final decision rests in the hands of co-ordinators.
  11. Any act of misbehaviour or misconduct will lead to immediate disqualification of the team.
  12. The robots are not allowed to use grippers or actuations which are intended to harm the opponent's robot.
  13. The competition is aimed at making the game a friendly football match rather than a robo war.
