DESCRIPTION

      Team of 2 and a guide sent into the room. There are various puzzles within the room which the contests have to figure out and solve to win the competition.

RULES

  1. Team event (2 persons per team).
  2. Consists of two rounds.
  3. First round is a quiz of 8 questions.
  4. Based on the result of first round the participants will be selected for second round.
  5. Round two consist of 4 tasks.
  6. Each contestants are required to complete all of the 4 tasks within the given time limit.
  7. Judgement will be based on time taken and completeness of each task.
