DESCRIPTION

      The event is conducted to pop up the layout designing skills of the participants. They are instructed to develop a PCB design of the given circuit diagram neatly.

RULES

  1. Participants are not permitted to have mobile phones and any other electronic equipments.
  2. Participants should plot the mirror image of PCB_layout of the given circuit diagram.
  3. Participants are allowed to have a black ball pen, scale, eraser & pencil.
  4. Each participant can't take more than three chances, negative marks will be awarded for the second and third submission.
  5. There will be elimination rounds if necessary.
  6. Circuit Diagram will be given on the spot.
  7. Duration of the event is two (2) hours.
  8. Judgement of the work will be based on how the layout is being designed (based on the basic concepts and rules of PCB layout designing), neatness, area used properly.
  9. The faster you finish the work neatly, the easier you will bag the prize.
  10. Any misbehavior during the event can lead to elimination of the team.
  11. Decision of the judges will be final and binding.
