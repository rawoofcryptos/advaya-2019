DESCRIPTION

      Circuiteers out there! Get ready for some electric moments this advaya. If your brain can resist the pressure and yet think quickly, you can make it to the top. Shock us with your skills and team spirit. Remember accuracy with speed is touchstone!.

RULES

  1. Team consist of 3 members.
  2. Participants should produce a valid identity card of their instistute.
  3. Cross college team are allowed.
  4. Conduct a MCQ test where questions will be general Electrical Engineering and logical reasoning.
  5. Time limit will be 45 minutes.
  6. Qualified teams will be selected for next level.
  7. Given a task, complete the given task as fast as possible.
  8. Hints will be provided if asked for, But points will be deducted for each hint provided.
  9. The team can ask for another task if they have completed the first one before the scheduled time, and the points for the second circuit will be double, third will be quardaple and so on.
  10. The components will be provided along with the task that given for you.
  11. Only completed circuits will be awarded with the points (Considering the performance of all teams).
  12. Speed and neatness will be considered
  13. Decision of the judges will be final and binding.
